General Usage

How can I register/sign in? +
To register, download the Spotlas app and follow the sign-up instructions. If you already have the app, go to the profile section and click "Login." There you can log in or register if you don't have an account yet.
How do I update my profile information? +
Go to your profile, click on "Settings," and select "Edit Profile." Here, you can change your name, profile picture, and other details.
How can I reset my password? +
Go to the login page and click "Forgot Password?" Follow the instructions to receive a password reset link via email.

Adding & Editing Places

How do I add a new place? +
Open the app, tap the plus icon, and select "Add a new place." Fill in the details and save the entry.
How can I edit or update a place? +
Open the place you want to edit and tap "Edit." Update the necessary details and save the changes.
Why was my added place not published? +
All new places are reviewed before being published. If your place wasn’t published, it might not meet our guidelines or could be missing essential information.

Maps & Navigation

How do I find places near me? +
Use the map feature and enable location access to discover places nearby.
Can I filter places by category? +
Yes, you can filter places by categories like restaurants, cafés, or landmarks. Use the search bar or filter options in the app.
How can I save or share places? +
Tap the save icon to add a place to your favorites. To share a place, use the share option and send it to friends.

Ratings & Comments

How can I leave a review or comment? +
Open the place, scroll down, and tap "Write a review." You can leave a star rating and add a comment.
Can I edit my own review later? +
Yes, you can edit or delete your review by going to your profile and viewing your previous reviews.
Why was my review removed? +
Reviews may be removed if they violate our guidelines, contain spam, or include inappropriate content.

Community & Interaction

How can I follow other users? +
Go to a user’s profile and tap "Follow" to see their posts and activities.
How do I report a post or comment? +
Tap the three dots next to a post or comment and select "Report" to flag inappropriate content.
Is there a way to send private messages? +
Currently, Spotlas does not offer direct messaging. However, you can share places or posts via external apps.

Technical Issues & Support

The app is not working properly – what should I do? +
Try restarting the app, updating to the latest version, or clearing the cache in the app settings.
How can I contact Spotlas support? +
Visit our support page or send an email to support@spotlas.com .
Is there a web version of Spotlas? +
Spotlas is currently available mainly as an app. A web version is planned for the future.

Privacy & Security

How can I delete my account? +
Go to settings, select "Delete Account," and follow the instructions.
What data does Spotlas store? +
Spotlas stores your profile data, location data, and app activities. For more details, check our privacy policy.
How can I manage my privacy settings? +
In the settings, you can adjust your privacy options and choose which information is publicly visible.
